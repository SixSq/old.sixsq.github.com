NuvlaBox is smart cloud-in-a-box appliance which offers easy and affordable access to cloud computing. It comes in two versions, standard and mini, both of which and can be customised with your own branding. The operating system is CentOS, OpenNebula provides the cloud layer and SlipStream is the PaaS.
