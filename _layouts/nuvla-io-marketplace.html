App Integration - Limited offer October only: free!

We are nearing the official launch date of the Nuvla.io Marketplace.

In preparation, we are offering to app vendors and start-ups free integration in October. This is the easiest way to take advantage of having your apps deployed at scale and in a click at the edge and in the cloud.

What do we expect from you?

  • Package you app as Docker containers.
  • Apps for the edge must be either a single Docker container, or Docker Compose or Docker Swarm.
  • Apps for the cloud can be as for the edge or Kubernetes Manifest (depends on what infrastructure your customers run)
  • We recommend you upload your images in a registry protected by username/password
  • Decide how much you want to charge for your app.
  • Decide if you want your apps to be public or private (you share them with your customers).

What can you expect from SixSq engineers?

  • We will dedicate up to 2 days of engineering support
  • Walk you through the process if you are not familiar with Nuvla.io
  • Certify you app on the Nuvla.io platform

What can you expect from Nuvla.io?

  • Nuvla.io meters hourly and invoices monthly
  • You get paid as soon as the customer pays
  • We take a 20% fee
  • We can give you promotional coupons to help you promote your apps to customers

Here are a few resources you might find helpful to familiarise yourself with Nuvla.io:

  • SixSq Classroom YouTube channel
  • Nuvla.io technical documentation website

You will also find Case Studies on our website: Smart City, Outpost and Field Clinics, Retail, Big Data Science.
