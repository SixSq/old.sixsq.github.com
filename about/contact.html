Directions:

It's easy to get to the SixSq offices. We are located about 15 minutes from Geneva International Airport and from Geneva Cornavin central railway station. To take public transport from the airport to the venue, you catch bus 23 (direction ZIPLO) or 28 (direction Parfumerie) to Blandonnet then change to tram 18 (direction CERN), getting off at the stop Bois-du-Lan. Once you get off the tram, you will see a brown building with the words “Geneux Dancet” in yellow lettering on the roof. The SixSq offices are located on the 3rd floor of this building.
