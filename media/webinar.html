Edge and Nuvla.io Webinar

What is the edge? Why it matters? And is there a simple, yet secure and scalable way to start my journey to the edge?

These are questions we will answer. This one hour webinar is the place to learn, interact and discover, with SixSq experts.

This webinar is composed of two main parts:

  • Introduction of the edge. What are the main criteria for choosing the edge? Examples where the edge makes a big difference and bring amazing outcomes (20 mins).
  • Hands-on demo of Nuvla.io, our SaaS edge management platform. This more technical part demystifies the edge, and walks you through a step by step demo of how to create an edge device, we call a NuvlaBox. And then how to manage it securely, how to deploy and update containerised applications in it (30 mins).

We will keep 10 minutes at the end to answer participants questions, unless we take the questions during the webinar.
