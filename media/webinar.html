Edge Computing and Nuvla.io Webinar

What is the edge? Why does it matter? And is there a simple, yet secure and scalable way to start your journey to the edge?

These are questions we will tackle during this one-hour webinar where you will learn, interact and discover, with SixSq experts.

This webinar is composed of two parts:

  1. Introduction to the edge. What are the main criteria for choosing the edge? Including examples where edge computing makes a big difference and brings amazing outcomes. (20 mins)
  2. Hands-on demo of Nuvla.io, our SaaS edge management platform. This more technical part demystifies the edge, and walks you through a step-by-step demo of how to configure an edge device. For the demo we use SixSq's NuvlaBox. You will also learn how to manage the device securely and how to deploy and update containerised AI applications within it. (30 mins)

Questions are welcome during the webinar and we will keep 10 minutes at the end to cover any additional queries.
