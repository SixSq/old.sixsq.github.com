About SixSq

At SixSq, we enable organisations to accelerate their business from the Edge to the Cloud. We are a fast-growing Software as a Service company, offering an Edge-to-Cloud agile, secure and open software platform (Nuvla.io) for the most demanding organisations. SixSq has an open and a highly collaborative culture, internally and externally with our customers & partners. We manage the entire organisation as an agile operation. We sell as a team. We believe that achieving “customer success” is our value “secret sauce” and is key for our growth.

Now part of the Ekinops Group, SixSq will benefit from an established organisation, with shared lead and opportunities, but also has its own roadmap and objectives.
