--- layout: category_index title: Research and Development category: r-and-d class: overview --- SixSq is at the frontier between innovative development and commercial exploitation. SixSq was born from ideas created at [CERN](http://cern.ch), the European Laboratory for Particle Physics. SixSq continues to participate in research and development projects, both at the national and international levels. We bring our expertise and experience to projects in which we participate while also taking advantage of the projects' international collaborations to refine and to expand our solutions: [SlipStream](/products/slipstream) and [NuvlaBox](/products/nuvlabox). The wider research community benefits from our commitment to open-source development and our [customers](/customers) benefit from the cutting-edge innovation coming from these projects.
